## One dimension
$$d(p,q) = |p - q|$$

## Two dimensions
$$d(p,q) = \sqrt{(p_1 - q_1)^2 + (p_2 - q_2)^2}$$

## Three dimensions

$$d(p,q) = \sqrt{(p_1 - q_1)^2 + (p_2 - q_2)^2 + (p_3 - q_3)^2}$$

## N dimensions

$$d(p,q) = \sqrt{(p_1 - q_1)^2 + (p_2 - q_2)^2 + ... +  (p_n - q_n)^2}$$
